Alif Alif Atoll

Maldives

Alif Alif Atoll, nestled within the breathtaking archipelago of the Maldives, captivates visitors with its pristine beauty and serene ambiance. Located in the heart of the Indian Ocean, this atoll is renowned for its mesmerizing turquoise waters, powdery white sand beaches, and lush tropical vegetation.

Maaya Thila

Thila is one of the most famous dive sites in the Maldives. It’s a submerged pinnacle teeming with marine life, including sharks, turtles, and colorful reef fish.

Ukulhas Island

Ukulhas is a local island known for its clean beaches and strong focus on environmental conservation.

Thoddoo Island

Thoddoo is famous for its agriculture, particularly for growing watermelons and papayas, and also has stunning beaches.

Rasdhoo Island

Rasdhoo is a popular destination for divers and snorkelers, with easy access to some of the best dive sites in the atoll. The island has a beautiful beach and offers a mix of local culture and tourism services.

Veligandu Island
