Jolla Cova

San Diego

Located in the vibrant city of San Diego, La Jolla Cove is a breathtaking gem nestled along the coast, renowned for its stunning natural beauty and abundant marine life. With its picturesque cliffs, pristine beaches, and clear azure waters, La Jolla Cove offers visitors a serene escape and a chance to immerse themselves in the wonders of the Pacific Ocean.

Cova d’en Xoroi

This iconic cave bar and nightclub is carved into the cliffs overlooking the Mediterranean Sea.

Cala en Porter Beach

Located near Cova d’en Xoroi, Cala en Porter is a stunning beach set in a sheltered bay with turquoise waters and soft sand. It’s perfect for swimming, sunbathing, and family-friendly activities.

Binibeca Vell

The highest point on the island of Menorca, Monte Toro offers panoramic views of the entire island. At the summit, you’ll find a 17th-century sanctuary and a statue of Christ. It’s a peaceful and scenic place to visit.

Grotta dell'Arpaia (Byron's Grotto)

Named after the Romantic poet Lord Byron, who famously swam in its waters, this sea cave is accessible by boat and is renowned for its crystalline waters and natural beauty..

Ciutadella de Menorca

The historic city of Ciutadella is filled with charming squares, beautiful churches, and narrow streets lined with old mansions.
