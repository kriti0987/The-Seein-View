Temple of the Sacred Heart of Jesus

Spain

The Temple of the Sacred Heart of Jesus, located in Barcelona, Spain, stands as a magnificent testament to Catalan modernist architecture. Designed by architect Enric Sagnier, construction of this awe-inspiring church commenced in 1902 and was completed in 1961. Perched atop the Tibidabo Mountain, the temple offers breathtaking panoramic views of Barcelona and the Mediterranean Sea. As a symbol of Barcelona's spiritual and architectural heritage, the Temple of the Sacred Heart of Jesus remains a cherished landmark, drawing visitors from around the world.

The Basilica Itself (Temple Expiatori del Sagrat Cor)

The basilica is a masterpiece of neo-Gothic architecture, with intricate details and beautiful stained-glass windows.

Tibidabo Amusement Park

Located next to the basilica, Tibidabo Amusement Park is one of the oldest in the world, offering classic rides and attractions.

Tibidabo Viewpoint

The Tibidabo viewpoint, located near the basilica, offers one of the most stunning views of Barcelona.

Sagrat Cor Interior Chapel

Inside the basilica, the lower chapel is worth exploring for its tranquil atmosphere and beautiful religious artwork. The chapel is a peaceful retreat from the hustle and bustle of the city, offering a moment of reflection.

Tibidabo Mountain Hiking Trails

The area around Mount Tibidabo offers several hiking and nature trails through the surrounding Collserola Natural Park.
